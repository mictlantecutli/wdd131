Typography

Playfair Display: Its classic and refined style complements Benji's art and adds a touch of class to the page. It will be used in headings and main titles.

Roboto: Ideal for body text and paragraphs. Its clarity and versatility ensure excellent readability, while maintaining a professional and clean appearance.

Montserrat: It is perfect for subtitles, buttons and highlights. Its clean and modern style brings a touch of dynamism to the design.
